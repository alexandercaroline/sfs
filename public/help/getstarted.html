Getting Started

Here you’ll find information about Smart Farmers' system and ways to get started on various sections

What is Smart Farmers' System?

Smart farmers’ system is a farm system that will be used by sellers (farmer) and buyers (farm produce entrepreneur) of their farm produce.
The system is meant to increase:

  1. Market Accessibility for farmers

  2. Reduce operation cost for taking goods to the physical market (Your market is just a click of a button away)

  3. Increase Transparency as You are able to communicate with Your clients in the platform.

How do i make an order?

  1. To make an order, Sign Up as a client.

  2. The system will redirect to Login page, enter the required details to successfully login.

  3. Following a successful login the system will redirect you to Client homepage.

  4. Click the Make order button on the menu-bar.
  5. Click, Submit to place your order

How do i post my products for sale?

  1. To post Your products for sale, Sign up as a farmer.

  2. The system will redirect you to Login page, enter the email and password

  3. Following successful login, the system will diresct you to Farmer homepage.

  4. Click, Place Order button on the menu-bar

  5. Click, Submit to post your order
